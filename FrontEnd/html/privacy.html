Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026. We are committed to protecting your privacy.

🔒 Information We Collect

Information You Provide

When you create an account, we collect your name and email address. When you use HabitzPro, we store the habits you create, your completion records, and your preferences.

Automatically Collected Information

We may collect basic usage data such as pages visited and features used, to improve the product. We do not use tracking cookies for advertising.

📄 How We Use Your Information

  • To provide, operate, and maintain HabitzPro
  • To personalize your experience and remember your preferences
  • To send important account and service notifications
  • To improve and develop new features based on usage patterns
  • To respond to your support requests and inquiries

🔐 Data Storage & Security

Currently, HabitzPro stores all your data locally in your browser using localStorage. This means your data stays on your device and is never transmitted to external servers without your consent.

We implement industry-standard security practices to protect any data processed by our services.

🤝 Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We do not display advertisements. Your habit data is yours.

We may share data only when required by law or to protect the rights and safety of our users.

👤 Your Rights

  • Access: You can access all your data within the app at any time
  • Delete: You can clear your data by clearing your browser's localStorage
  • Correction: You can edit your habits and profile information at any time
  • Contact: Reach us at privacy@habitzpro.com for any privacy concerns

📞 Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at privacy@habitzpro.com or visit our Contact page.
