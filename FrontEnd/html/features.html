What We Offer

Everything You Need to
Build Lasting Habits

Powerful features designed to help you stay consistent, celebrate progress, and grow every day.

✅ Habit Tracking

Create and manage unlimited habits with custom emojis, categories, and personal notes. Check off habits daily, build streaks, and watch your consistency grow over time.

  • 40+ emoji icons to personalize each habit
  • 5 categories: Health, Mind, Lifestyle, Work, Social
  • Add personal notes and goals to each habit
  • Edit or delete habits at any time

🔥 Streaks & Goals

Nothing is more motivating than a streak. HabitzPro automatically calculates your current streak for every habit, encouraging you to keep going day after day.

  • Auto-calculated streaks from your real completion history
  • Set custom day goals per habit (e.g. 30-day challenge)
  • Progress bar shows exactly how close you are to your goal

📊 Analytics & Insights

Get a real picture of your progress with charts powered by your actual habit data — no fake numbers, ever.

  • Daily, weekly, and monthly bar charts
  • Completion rate donut chart
  • This week at a glance view
  • Best streak tracking across all habits

🏆 Achievements

Unlock milestone badges as you build your habit practice. From your very first habit to legendary 30-day streaks — every win is celebrated.

  • 8 unique milestone badges to unlock
  • Tap completed habits for a streak celebration popup
  • Daily completed habits highlighted in one place

📋 Habit Tracking Chart

A beautiful monthly calendar grid — just like the habit trackers you see in journals and planners — but digital and always with you.

  • Full month view with week columns
  • Click to tick any day manually
  • Set your personal goal per habit
  • Navigate between months to review past progress

🌙 Light & Dark Mode

Choose between a fresh green light theme or a sleek purple dark mode. Your preference is saved and applied across all pages instantly.
